Synopsis

Sensor Ship voyages.

 

Introductory description

'Sensor Ship', a short animation based on poetry, is a type of non-narrative experimental animation in which each scene and audio are not directly related to each other. Rather than connecting all the scenes and understanding them together, it is made to feel each shot and each sound as they are.

 

Director's statement

Most of us acquire information through several media such as newspapers, magazines, books, radios, television, movies, and the Internet that have already been censored. And this censored information enters into our unconscious mind and becomes our thoughts. But can we know and perceive that these thoughts are different from those created by our own experience and witness? Are we aware that many of the thoughts within us that we believe to be true may, in fact, be indoctrinated?
